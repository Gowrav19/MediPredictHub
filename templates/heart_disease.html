Understanding Heart Disease Risk

Heart disease is the leading cause of death worldwide. Early detection and prevention can significantly reduce your risk of cardiovascular complications.

Coronary Artery Disease: Blocked arteries
Heart Failure: Weakened heart muscle
Arrhythmia: Irregular heartbeat

Risk Factors

  • Age (men ≥45, women ≥55)
  • Family history of heart disease
  • High blood pressure (≥140/90)
  • High cholesterol (≥240 mg/dL)
  • Smoking or tobacco use
  • Diabetes or prediabetes
  • Obesity (BMI ≥30)
  • Physical inactivity
  • Chronic stress
  • Unhealthy diet
